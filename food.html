There are many foods that originated in Spain

When you visit Spain, these are the meals to order

One of the most popular dishes is the paella

Best tip is to try the mixed seafood paella to try the true Spanish cuisine

Here is a simple paella recipe to try at home

Ingredients Method
1tbsp olive oil 1. Heat the oil in a larger frying pan or wok.
1 onion Add the onion and soften for 5 mins.
1 tsp smoked paprika and dried thyme Stir in the paprika, thyme and rice, stir for 1 min, then slash in the wine.
300g paella rice Once it has evaporated, stir in the tomatoes and stock.
3 tbsp white wine Season and cook, uncovered, for about 15 mins, stirring now and again
400g can chopped tomatoes with garlic until rise is almost tender and still surrounded with some liquid.
900ml chicken stock
400g bag frozen seafood mix 2. Stir the frozen seafood into the pan and cover with a lid.
juice 1/2 lemon Simmer for 5 mins, or until the prawns are cooked through and the rice is tender.
handful flat-leaf parsely Squeeze over the lemon juice, scatter with parsley and serve with extra lemon wedges.
